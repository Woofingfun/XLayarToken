How It Works?

Launch your meme coin on X Layer with our Meme Coin Launchpad! It’s simple and fast:

  • Connect Your Wallet: Use MetaMask to connect to X Layer (chain ID: 196). Ensure you have enough OKB for gas fees.
  • Create Your Meme Coin: Enter a name (e.g., “XCoin”), symbol (e.g., “XCN”), and total supply. Tokens are minted to your wallet with 18 decimals.
  • View Your Meme Coins: Fetch the latest 100 coins you’ve created, including addresses, names, and symbols.
  • Explore Latest Coins: View the latest 100 meme coins created by all users on the platform.
  • Fair & Secure: Built on X Layer for low fees and secure token creation.
